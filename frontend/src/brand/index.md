## Quick Reference

### Brand Position

> Soul Miner's Eden is a **regenerative demonstration farm and content studio** that sells beauty, trust, and story first — and products second.

### One-Sentence Description

> "Soul Miner's Eden is a 65-acre regenerative demonstration farm in Bogart, Georgia, turning compost, silvopasture, and livestock into a camera-ready destination for Athens families and Southland Organics customers."

### Priority Stack

1. **Beautiful** — if it doesn't make the property more beautiful, we don't do it
2. **Easy** — simple, self-serve, honor-based
3. **Marketing stage** — everything is filmable content
4. **Profitable** — the farm sustains itself

### Core Pillars

- **Beautiful** — photo-worthy, "weekend destination" not "production farm"
- **Easeful** — simple systems, honor-based, no clutter
- **Regenerative** — one soil-health story connecting everything
- **Premium & Honest** — fewer SKUs, high prices, total transparency

### Tagline

> "A regenerative farm in Bogart, Georgia."

### Voice in One Line

Calm, confident, slightly poetic but plainspoken. Faith-rooted but never preachy.

### Primary Colors

- **Deep Charcoal** (#1c1917) — text, line art
- **Warm Cream** (#fafaf9) — backgrounds
- **Terracotta** (#b59289) — warmth, accents
- **Muted Green** (#16a34a) — CTAs, growth

### Typography

- **Display**: Poppins (bold, confident)
- **Serif**: Playfair Display (poetic, invitational)
- **Body**: Inter (clean, readable)

### Key Differentiators

1. Georgia's ONLY regenerative organic compost — zero competitors within 60 miles
2. 65-acre working demonstration farm
3. Faith-driven stewardship
4. Honor-system farm stand
5. Camera-ready proof that Southland biology works
6. Not just a supplier — a destination
